Description

The M3 is a legend in the world of performance cars, giving enthusiasts reason to speak in gushing superlatives whenever given the chance. Under the hood is a twin-turbo 3.0-liter inline-six that makes 425 hp and 406 lb-ft of torque; it makes glorious sounds all the way to its 7500-rpm redline. A six-speed manual is standard and a seven-speed dual-clutch automatic is optional. So you know, the M3 badge is affixed only to sedan models; coupe and convertible models wear the M4 designation.
